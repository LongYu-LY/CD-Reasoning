# CD-Reasoning
The accurate measurement of perceptual color dif
ferences (CDs) between two images plays a important role in
 modern smartphone photography. While traditional CD metrics
 offer numerical scores to quantify color discrepancies, they fall
 short of providing intuitive insights or explanations about the
 factors that align with human perception and reasoning. Here,
 we present CD-Reasoning, an innovative method designed not
 merely to compute numerical CD scores but also to provide
 a detailed rationale for the observed CDs between images.
 This method surpasses simple numerical quantification, deliv
ering a more profound and explanatory analysis that bridges
 quantitative assessments with the qualitative, cognitive reasoning
 characteristic of human perception.
